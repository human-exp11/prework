Bio

Mac MIller was born in Pittsburgh "The Burgh" PA. He became famous off his mixtapes, "KIDS" and "Best Day Ever." In September of 2018, Mac died from an accidental overdose of cocaine, fentanyl, and alcohol. He was just 26 years old.
